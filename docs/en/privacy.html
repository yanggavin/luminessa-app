Privacy Policy

Luminessa does not collect, store, or transmit your photos, videos, or personal information to our servers. Camera preview, LUT rendering, capture, and export are processed locally on your device.

We use standard Apple APIs such as UserDefaults to store app preferences locally on your device.

Subscriptions

When you purchase Luminessa Pro, Apple processes payment. RevenueCat verifies subscription status so Luminessa can unlock Pro features.

We do not send your photos, videos, camera frames, or location tags to RevenueCat.

Permissions

  • Camera: Required to capture photos and record video.
  • Microphone: Required only when you record video with audio.
  • Location: Used only when you turn on photo location tagging.
  • Photo Library: Required to save captures and exports to your device.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@luminessa-app.newtree.tech.
